PROGRESS

SHOW STATEMENT

Stepping into 2021 as graduates could not have been more daunting. The pandemic created chaos and uncertainty that hung over our heads for the past year. But at the end of our journey as students and our emergence into our new lives, we have chosen celebration.

We choose to celebrate a world without rhythm. A world that values improvisation, intuition, and flexibility. A world we call Freeform.

Join us as we break the borders of our reality by exploring media ranging from painting, drawing, sculpture, to video, animation, mixed-media, and augmented reality. The first group to graduate with the new program name, MHC Art & Design, this year’s graduating class truly embraces the program’s focus on blending fine arts and design. Combining hands-on processes with digital techniques creates more opportunities and a greater depth of understanding, allowing graduates to apply a wider array of skills in the contemporary workspace and the ever-growing marketplace of art and design.

This exhibition promotes and celebrates not only the individual artist but the collaboration between them. The work displayed in the gallery was created by individual artists, but the concept, design, and marketing of the exhibition was entirely collaborative. Together we aimed to encapsulate our collective experience, achievement, and progression, of reaching new heights and embracing who we are. We align ourselves with our past and our present, acknowledging who we were before we came here, and the possibilities that await us. We now know that without the guidance and knowledge from our instructors, counsellors, staff members, classmates, friends and family—we may not have gotten this far. It is with their help and the individual practices of each graduate that we are able to work towards the greatness of our future.

Today, we toast to Freeform!
