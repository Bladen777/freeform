PROGRESS

TYREL ANDERSON

Growing up in small-town Gull Lake, Sk — I never thought that I would have chosen a career path in the arts. Before attending the Art and Design program at Medicine Hat College, I worked for five years to figure out what I wanted to do, moving around as I went. It wasn’t until I moved to Saskatoon that I found where my true passion lies. While in Saskatoon, I decided to create my clothing company which led me to learn graphic design and photography. Not long after this, I applied to the Art and Design Program. While in school, I have focused on photography, videography, graphic design, and motion graphics. I have concentrated more on photography and videography on my freelance work but have been implementing my knowledge of motion graphics and design in my branding and other work. Being so diverse, I struggle with what I want to fill focus on, so my goal is to start my firm to incorporate all of my skills that I have learned and continue to grow them.
