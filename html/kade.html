KADE RUTT

Kade Rutt has been, for as long as they can remember, creating and making stuff. They are an artist first, cosplayer second! They work in many mediums, from hands on sculpture, working in props and costume design, all the way to digital arts and character design. They are inspired by video games, anime, and Dungeons & Dragons. They experiment a lot with their work and put lots of time into making their ideas come to life. Each piece is double checked and approved by their cat Molly.
