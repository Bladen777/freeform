MCCLAY BOS

McClay Bos decided after high school to go to Medicine Hat College for Art and Design. Here she discovered a deep passion for many new forms of both fine arts and design. She is most interested in 3D design, sculpting, intaglio, and illustration. Her work is about pushing herself and others into finding new ways to look at everyday life, and into connecting things that normally are not connected. After graduation, she is planning to travel and learn new skills to add to her passion for art.
