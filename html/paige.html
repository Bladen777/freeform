PAIGE COOPER

Paige Cooper was born and raised in Medicine Hat, Alberta. To pursue her passion for both fine arts and basketball, she extended her studies in Medicine Hat College’s Art and Design program while playing for the MHC Women’s Basketball team. Her art is strongly influenced by family and nostalgia, surfacing in multiple past and present works. Post graduation, Cooper will further her work experience and potential future schooling in Edmonton, Alberta.
