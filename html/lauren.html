LAUREN FRIESEN

Lauren Friesen is a 21-year-old student born and raised in Medicine Hat, AB. Over her time spent as an art and design student she has grown a strong passion in design. Some of her favourite disciplines consist of book design, typography, interactive media, and digital art. Much of her inspiration comes from popular culture and net art which has led her to explore a variety of digital mediums and subjects throughout her work. Her goals for the future are to continue learning and testing her abilities in a variety of fields in hopes to become a well-rounded creative, as her personal brand “Looking>Forward” speaks for.
