PROGRESS

ARBËRESHA SALIHU

Arberesha Salihu is a 23-year-old international student living in Medicine Hat who was born and raised in Gjilan, Kosovo. In 2015 she decided to move to Canada to pursue a career in Art and Design at Medicine Hat College to experience a new chapter in life. She is passionate about traditional art; especially painting, human body drawing, lithography, and screen printing. She creates work that is influenced by her Balkan culture, childhood experience, and personality. Design is also a big influence in her work which includes typography, illustration, design, and photography. Her work is mostly inspired by feminist artists and designers such as Paula Scher, Judy Chicago, Frida Khalo, and Artemisia Gentileschi.
