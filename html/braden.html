PROGRESS

BRADEN KOWALCHUK

Born and raised in the Hafford, SK area, I was privileged to learn and use many practical tools and techniques related to living in a rural environment. I always had an interest in art, but, out of high school I chose to learn about CAD/CAM at Sask Polytech. After a few years I learned that it wasn't the right fit and chose a different path, I worked at the Saskatoon Scotiabank Theatre, and then at Saskatoon Fire and Flood. I then got the opportunity to be an electrician which I did for a couple of years before realizing that the thing that was missing in my life was art. Hence, I moved to Medicine Hat and found my niche in sculpture. I absolutely love to employ mechanical elements, as well as natural accents in all of my pieces.
